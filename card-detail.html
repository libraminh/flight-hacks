How Does The Qantas Premier Platinum Bonus Points Work?

To earn a total of 120,000 bonus points, you will need to spend $1,500 or more on eligible purchases per month for the first 6 months. Every month you complete the minimum spend requirement, you will earn 20,000 bonus Qantas Points. The bonus Qantas Points will ordinarily be credited within 6 – 8 weeks of meeting the spend criteria for that month. Keep in mind this needs to be in consecutive order so you can’t just skip a month and pick up where you left off later.

The months are based on your statement period and not calendar months; this means you have until the date listed on your statement to meet the minimum spend.

Qantas Lounge Invites

As part of this offer, members will also receive 2 Qantas lounge invitations each year they hold the card. These can be redeemed for domestic Qantas Club visits and international Qantas Business class lounge access. You can use them as long as you’re flying Qantas or Jetstar. To use your digital passes, you can simply link them to your Qantas flight booking online via this website. Just make sure you do this at least 24hrs before departure!

Qantas Perk: Discounted Companion Fare

The Qantas Premier Platinum card comes with a range of Qantas specific perks, one of those is the companion fare discount when you fly with Qantas.

Here’s the deal; once a year you can take up to 20% off selected domestic flights for you and up to 8 companions when you book directly via your Qantas Premier Platinum Concierge. The discount works in tiers; Red eDeal fares receive a 10% discount, Flex Economy 15% and Business 20%.

To book your discounted companion fares, simply call the Qantas Premier Concierge on 1300 992 700 anytime, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
